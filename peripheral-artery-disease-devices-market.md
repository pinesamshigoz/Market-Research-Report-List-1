<p><p>The Peripheral Artery Disease Devices market encompasses various products used to treat narrowed or blocked peripheral arteries. Stents are mesh tubes inserted to keep arteries open. Angioplasty balloon catheters are inflated to widen artery segments. Drug-eluting balloons release medication to prevent re-narrowing. Atherectomy devices remove plaque from arteries. Other devices may include imaging tools or vascular grafts, enhancing diagnostic and treatment options for patients with peripheral artery disease, thus improving blood flow and overall outcomes.</p></p>
