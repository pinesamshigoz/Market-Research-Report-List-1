<p><p>The metalworking lubricant market encompasses various types tailored for specific processes, including cutting, rolling, and contour machining. Cutting lubricants reduce friction and dissipate heat during machining operations, enhancing tool life and ensuring smoother finishes. Rolling lubricants provide a protective film that minimizes wear on rollers and improves surface quality in forming processes. Contour machining lubricants are designed for complex shapes, ensuring precision while preventing overheating and tool degradation. Each type optimizes performance, stability, and efficiency in metalworking applications.</p></p>
