<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The energy-efficient powder coatings market comprises two primary types: thermoplastic and thermosetting coatings. Thermoplastic coatings can be melted and reformed, allowing for easy recycling and minimal waste, making them environmentally friendly. In contrast, thermosetting coatings undergo a chemical change during curing, resulting in a hard and durable finish that offers superior resistance to heat and chemicals. Both types contribute to energy efficiency by reducing energy consumption during application and curing processes, while also promoting sustainability in coating practices.</p></p>
