<p><strong>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) Diamond Pad Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plated Type</li><li>Brazed Type</li><li>Sintered Type</li><li>CVD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) diamond pad regulator market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Plated Type**: Features a thin layer of diamond particles electroplated onto a substrate, offering cost-effectiveness for softer materials.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Brazed Type**: Involves bonding diamond particles using a brazing process, providing durability and better performance on harder substrates.</p><p>3. **Sintered Type**: Diamond particles are fused under high pressure and temperature, resulting in robust pads with extended lifespan.</p><p>4. **CVD Type**: Utilizes chemical vapor deposition to create diamond films, delivering superior uniformity and performance across various materials.</p></p>
