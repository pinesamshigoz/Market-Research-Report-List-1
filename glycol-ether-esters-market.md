<p><ul><li>E-series</li><li>P-series</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glycol ether esters are categorized primarily into two market types: E-series and P-series. The E-series, derived from ethylene glycol, is characterized by its low volatility and high solvency, making it suitable for paints and coatings. In contrast, the P-series, derived from propylene glycol, offers lower toxicity and is preferred in applications like cleaning agents and personal care products. Both series cater to distinct industrial needs, balancing performance characteristics, environmental impact, and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
