<p><p>Iron deficiency anemia therapy encompasses multiple approaches. Oral iron therapy involves administering iron supplements via mouth, which is often the first-line treatment due to ease of use and cost-effectiveness. Parenteral iron therapy involves intravenous or intramuscular iron administration, suitable for patients who cannot tolerate oral supplements or require rapid correction. Red blood cell transfusions provide an immediate increase in hemoglobin levels for severe cases. Other therapies might include dietary modifications or treatments targeting underlying causes of anemia, ensuring a comprehensive approach to management.</p></p>
