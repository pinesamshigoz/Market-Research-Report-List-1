<p><p>Bone densitometers (BDM) are specialized devices used to measure bone mineral density, aiding in the diagnosis of osteoporosis and assessing fracture risk. The market for BDM is primarily segmented into axial and peripheral types. Axial BDMs, such as dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DXA) machines, assess central skeletal bones, providing accurate results for diagnosing bone conditions. Peripheral BDMs focus on measuring bone density in peripheral sites like the wrist or heel, offering a cost-effective and portable alternative for screening in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
