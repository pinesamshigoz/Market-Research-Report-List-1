<p><p>The engine oil market comprises primarily two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil, derived from refining crude oil, offers adequate lubrication and is often more affordable. It is widely used in older vehicle models. In contrast, synthetic oil is engineered through chemical processes, providing superior performance, higher resistance to breakdown, and better high-temperature stability. Synthetic oils are ideal for modern engines and demanding driving conditions, leading to enhanced fuel efficiency and engine longevity.</p></p>
