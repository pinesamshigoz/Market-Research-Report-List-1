<p><p>The automotive carbon ceramic brake system market can be categorized into single disc and multiple disc brake systems. Single disc brakes feature one brake disc per wheel, offering simplicity and adequate performance for most vehicles. In contrast, multiple disc brake systems utilize several discs layered together, enhancing stopping power and heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance and luxury vehicles. Both systems benefit from lightweight materials and reduced wear, improving overall braking efficiency and longevity in various driving conditions.</p></p>
