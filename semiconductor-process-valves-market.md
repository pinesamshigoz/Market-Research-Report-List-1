<p><p>The semiconductor process valves market encompasses various valve types essential for controlling fluid and gas flow in manufacturing processes. Diaphragm valves are used for precise regulation, while bellows valves provide hermetic sealing. Vacuum valves are critical for maintaining low-pressure environments. Ball valves offer quick on/off control, and butterfly valves are efficient for larger flow rates. Gate valves are designed for complete shut-off, angle valves facilitate directional flow changes, and Teflon valves provide chemical resistance. Other specialized valves serve niche applications.</p></p>
