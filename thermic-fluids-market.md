<p><p>The thermic fluids market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. Mineral oils are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and good thermal conductivity. Silicone fluids offer high thermal stability and resistance to oxidation, making them ideal for demanding environments. Aromatic fluids are known for their superior thermal performance but may have environmental concerns. Polyalkylene glycols (PAG) provide excellent heat transfer properties and are often biodegradable, while glycol-based fluids are known for their low freezing points and versatility across various industries.</p></p>
