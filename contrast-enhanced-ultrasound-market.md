<p><strong>The Contrast-Enhanced Ultrasound Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-targeted contrast-enhanced ultrasound</li><li>Targeted contrast-enhanced ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contrast-enhanced ultrasound market can be divided into two main types: non-targeted and targeted contrast-enhanced ultrasound. Non-targeted contrast-enhanced ultrasound utilizes contrast agents that enhance the overall imaging of organs or tissues, improving visibility without specificity. This approach is useful for general assessments. Conversely, targeted contrast-enhanced ultrasound employs specific contrast agents designed to bind to particular tissues or biomarkers, allowing for precise imaging of specific pathologies, thereby enhancing diagnostic accuracy and enabling better treatment planning.</p></p>
