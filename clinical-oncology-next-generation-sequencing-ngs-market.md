<p><p>The Clinical Oncology Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) market encompasses several types, including NGS Pre-Sequencing, Sequencing, and NGS Data Analysis. Pre-Sequencing involves sample preparation and library construction. Sequencing refers to the actual reading of DNA sequences to identify genomic alterations. NGS Data Analysis is subdivided into Primary Analysis (initial data processing), Secondary Analysis (variant calling and characterization), and Tertiary Analysis (clinical interpretation and integration with clinical data) to provide insights for personalized cancer treatment strategies.</p></p>
