<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) Brush market comprises two primary types: roll shape and sheet shape. Roll shape brushes are designed for efficient continuous use, often preferred in automated CMP processes for their flexibility and ease of application. Sheet shape brushes serve as flat, rigid tools that can provide uniform pressure and surface contact during planarization. Both types are essential for achieving high-quality surface finishes in semiconductor and microelectronics manufacturing, contributing to optimal performance and yield.</p></p>
